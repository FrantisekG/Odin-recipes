Fish and pasta, yes sir!

Cheap but actually not that bad!

This tuna spaghetti is great for poor people and students who are on a budget.

A really great thing about this food is, that it is literally inflation-proof.

Ingedients:

  • Spaghetti,
  • Tuna,
  • Tomato souce,
  • Onion & garlic,
  • Salt, pepper.

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salty watter for 10 minutes,
  2. Cut onion and add into pan on medium heat. Stir for 2-3 minutes,
  3. Add tomato souce and tuna into pan. Remove spaghetti from heat,
  4. Mix spaghetti into souce pan and stir,
  5. Serve with a touch of parmesan and fresh pepper.

Enjoy your meal!
